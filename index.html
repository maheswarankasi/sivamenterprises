Our Services

Keep your kitchen fresh and oil-free with our professional chimney services. We specialize in installation, deep cleaning, motor repair, and duct maintenance for all chimney models. Our skilled team delivers mess-free service and lasting results, ensuring your appliance performs at its best. Experience reliable care at the best prices.

Professional Chimney Installation

Correct installation is crucial for the performance and longevity of your appliance. We handle the entire setup process with precision.

  • Site Inspection: We assess your kitchen layout to recommend the best placement.
  • Ducting Solutions: Expert drilling and duct pipe installation for optimal airflow.
  • All Models Supported: Expertise in installing Wall-mounted, Island, and Corner chimneys from all major brands.
  • Safety Checks: Post-installation testing to ensure zero leakage and vibration.
Deep Chimney Cleaning & Services

Oil, grease, and grime can clog your chimney, reducing suction power and increasing fire risks. Our deep cleaning service restores your chimney to like-new condition.

  • Filter Cleaning: Intensive scrubbing of baffle and mesh filters to remove stubborn grease.
  • Motor Servicing: Cleaning the blower and motor assembly for smooth, noise-free operation.
  • Exterior Polishing: Wiping down the hood and exterior surfaces for a sparkling finish.
  • Dust Cleaning: Removing blockages from the duct pipe to improve suction.
Gas Pipeline Installation & Services

Ensuring your kitchen is safe is our top priority. From new installations to emergency repairs, we provide certified gas pipeline solutions using high-quality materials to prevent leaks and ensure consistent gas flow.

  • New Pipeline Installation: Precision fitting of high-grade Copper and GI pipes for independent houses and apartments.
  • Pipeline Extension: Extending or shifting existing gas points to perfectly suit your new modular kitchen layout.
  • Spares Replacement: Replacing worn-out valves, regulators, and ball valves with genuine ISI-marked components.
